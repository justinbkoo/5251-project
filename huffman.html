    How to interpret this tree:
  • Numbers inside leaf nodes denote the frequency of its character
  • Numbers inside non-leaf nodes denote the total frequency of all leaf nodes below.
  • Characters below leaf nodes denote the character it encodes.
  • Binary strings below the character denote the encoding of that character.
  • Elements of the visualization may overlap due to the nature of the input

The Knuth Algorithm was used to generate this visualization of the tree.
